## Overview
MilletBowl is a health-focused startup idea that delivers freshly cooked, personalized millet-based meals for diabetic patients, elderly people, and busy professionals. The concept focuses on disease control through traditional Indian millets and organic food instead of medication.

## Problem
Modern lifestyles, processed foods, and busy schedules are increasing diabetes, obesity, and digestive disorders. People lack convenient healthy meal options.

## Solution
MilletBowl provides:
- Personalized millet-based diet plans
- Fresh, hygienic meals delivered on time
- Subscription-based healthy food service
- Use of traditional grains like ragi, jowar, foxtail millet, barnyard millet, kodo millet, etc.

## Target Customers
- Diabetic patients
- Elderly individuals
- Working professionals
- Health-conscious people

## Business Model
- Weekly and monthly subscriptions
- Online orders via delivery platforms
- Corporate meal partnerships (future)
